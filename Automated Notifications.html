Automated Notifications

Feature Overview

Our automated notification system is designed to enhance communication and improve user engagement. It covers:

  • Reservation Reminders: Notify users with upcoming reservation details, ensuring they are prepared and informed.
  • Due Date Alerts: Send friendly alerts a few days before books are due to encourage timely returns.
  • Overdue Notices: Prompt notifications for overdue items, providing details about return procedures and avoiding confusion.
  • Customizable Settings: Allows users to choose their preferred notification methods (email, SMS, or app notifications).
  • Admin Dashboard: A streamlined interface for library staff to monitor, manage, and schedule notifications efficiently.

Why Choose Automated Notifications? This feature ensures seamless communication between library staff and users, fostering a better borrowing experience and minimizing overdue penalties.
